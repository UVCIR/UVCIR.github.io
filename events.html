UVCIR Events

Silent Auction and Online Fundraiser

We are collaborating with the Oxfam Club at Dartmouth to host a silent auction fundraiser to support refugees and immigrants on a global and local scale, as well as a research project abroad. By combining these three components, we hope to deliver our message loud and clear: that the larger Upper Valley and Dartmouth community stands in solidarity with refugees and immigrants close and far.

40% of the proceeds from both the silent auction and online fundraiser will go to Oxfam International, 40% to the International Institute of New Hampshire , and 20% to the Greek Forum of Refugees.

This event will take place on April 19th 8-10pm at Dartmouth College, Collis Common Ground. Entry is $3. Your help would be invaluable to reaching our goal of $5,000!
